1) Ensure Ambari has created/rotated the client mTLS Secret:
   kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} get secret {{ .Values.clientMtls.secretName }}

2) Serving TLS:
   - mode={{ .Values.serverTls.mode }}
     * cert-manager: CABundle auto-injected via annotation.
     * openshift:    CABundle injected by cluster.
     * external:     provide .Values.webhook.caBundle.

3) Scope is controlled by selectors:
   ns: {{ .Values.selectors.namespace.key }}={{ .Values.selectors.namespace.value }}
   pod: {{ .Values.selectors.object.key }}={{ .Values.selectors.object.value }}
